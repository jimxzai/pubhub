# 附錄一：腓利門書全文
**Philemon: Full Text (Chinese-English-Greek Parallel)**

---

## 問安 (Greeting) — 門 1-3

| 節 | 和合本修訂版 | ESV | 關鍵希臘詞 |
|---|-------------|-----|-----------|
| 1 | 為基督耶穌被囚的保羅，同兄弟提摩太寫信給我們所親愛的同工腓利門 | Paul, a prisoner for Christ Jesus, and Timothy our brother, To Philemon our beloved fellow worker | δέσμιος (desmios) = 囚犯 |
| 2 | 和妹子亞腓亞、並與我們同當兵的亞基布，以及在你家的教會 | and Apphia our sister and Archippus our fellow soldier, and the church in your house | ἐκκλησία (ekklesia) = 教會 |
| 3 | 願恩惠、平安從神我們的父和主耶穌基督歸與你們！ | Grace to you and peace from God our Father and the Lord Jesus Christ. | χάρις (charis) = 恩典 |

## 感恩與代求 (Thanksgiving & Prayer) — 門 4-7

| 節 | 和合本修訂版 | ESV |
|---|-------------|-----|
| 4 | 我禱告的時候提到你，常為你感謝我的神 | I thank my God always when I remember you in my prayers |
| 5 | 因聽說你的愛心並你向主耶穌和眾聖徒的信心 | because I hear of your love and of the faith that you have toward the Lord Jesus and for all the saints |
| 6 | 願你與人所同有的信心顯出功效，使人知道你們各樣善事都是為基督做的 | and I pray that the sharing of your faith may become effective for the full knowledge of every good thing that is in us for the sake of Christ |
| 7 | 兄弟啊，我為你的愛心大有快樂，大得安慰，因眾聖徒的心從你得了暢快 | For I have derived much joy and comfort from your love, my brother, because the hearts of the saints have been refreshed through you |

---

## 為阿尼西母代求 (Appeal for Onesimus) — 門 8-21

| 節 | 和合本修訂版 | ESV | 關鍵希臘詞 |
|---|-------------|-----|-----------|
| 8 | 我雖然靠著基督能放膽吩咐你合宜的事 | Accordingly, though I am bold enough in Christ to command you to do what is required | παρρησία (parrēsia) = 放膽 |
| 9 | 然而像我這有年紀的保羅，現在又是為基督耶穌被囚的，寧可憑著愛心求你 | yet for love's sake I prefer to appeal to you—I, Paul, an old man and now a prisoner also for Christ Jesus | ἀγάπη (agapē) = 愛 |
| 10 | 就是為我在捆鎖中所生的兒子阿尼西母求你 | I appeal to you for my child, Onesimus, whose father I became in my imprisonment | τέκνον (teknon) = 孩子 |
| 11 | 他從前與你沒有益處，但如今與你我都有益處 | Formerly he was useless to you, but now he is indeed useful to you and to me | ἄχρηστος/εὔχρηστος = 無益/有益 |
| 12 | 我現在打發他回你那裡去，他是我心上的人 | I am sending him back to you, sending my very heart | σπλάγχνα (splanchna) = 心腸 |
| 13 | 我本來有意將他留下，在我為福音所受的捆鎖中替你伺候我 | I would have been glad to keep him with me, in order that he might serve me on your behalf during my imprisonment for the gospel | διακονέω (diakoneō) = 服事 |
| 14 | 但不知道你的意見，我就不願意這樣行，叫你的善行不是出於勉強，乃是出於甘心 | but I preferred to do nothing without your consent in order that your goodness might not be by compulsion but of your own accord | ἑκούσιον (hekousion) = 甘心 |
| 15 | 他暫時離開你，或者是叫你永遠得著他 | For this perhaps is why he was parted from you for a while, that you might have him back forever | αἰώνιον (aiōnion) = 永遠 |
| 16 | 不再是奴僕，乃是高過奴僕，是親愛的兄弟——在我實在是如此，何況在你呢！不但在肉身上是如此，在主裡面更是如此 | no longer as a bondservant but more than a bondservant, as a beloved brother—especially to me, but how much more to you, both in the flesh and in the Lord | ἀδελφὸν ἀγαπητόν = 親愛的兄弟 |
| 17 | 你若以我為同伴，就收納他，如同收納我一樣 | So if you consider me your partner, receive him as you would receive me | κοινωνόν (koinōnon) = 同伴 |
| 18 | 他若虧負你，或欠你甚麼，都歸在我的賬上 | If he has wronged you at all, or owes you anything, charge that to my account | ἐλλόγα (elloga) = 歸在賬上 |
| 19 | 我保羅親筆寫的，我必償還——我並不用對你說，連你自己也是虧欠於我 | I, Paul, write this with my own hand: I will repay it—to say nothing of your owing me even your own self | ἀποτίσω (apotisō) = 必償還 |
| 20 | 兄弟啊，望你使我在主裡因你得快樂，使我的心在基督裡得暢快 | Yes, brother, I want some benefit from you in the Lord. Refresh my heart in Christ | ἀνάπαυσον (anapuson) = 使暢快 |
| 21 | 我寫信給你，深信你必順服，知道你所要行的必過於我所說的 | Confident of your obedience, I write to you, knowing that you will do even more than I say | ὑπακοή (hypakoē) = 順服 |

## 結語 (Conclusion) — 門 22-25

| 節 | 和合本修訂版 | ESV |
|---|-------------|-----|
| 22 | 此外你還要給我預備住處；因為我盼望藉著你們的禱告，必蒙恩到你們那裡去 | At the same time, prepare a guest room for me, for I am hoping that through your prayers I will be graciously given to you |
| 23-24 | 為基督耶穌與我同坐監的以巴弗問你安。我的同工馬可、亞里達古、底馬、路加也都問你安 | Epaphras, my fellow prisoner in Christ Jesus, sends greetings to you, and so do Mark, Aristarchus, Demas, and Luke, my fellow workers |
| 25 | 願我們主耶穌基督的恩常在你們的心裡！阿們 | The grace of the Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit |
