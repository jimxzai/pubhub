\newpage

\vspace*{2in}

\begin{center}

{\large\itshape 獻給}

\vspace{0.5cm}

{\Large\bfseries 每一個曾經是「沒有益處」的人}

\vspace{0.3cm}

{\normalsize\itshape To everyone who has ever been "useless"}

\vspace{1cm}

{\large 願你在基督裡找到新的身份，}

{\large 成為「親愛的兄弟」。}

\vspace{0.5cm}

{\normalsize\itshape May you find your new identity in Christ,}

{\normalsize\itshape and become a "beloved brother."}

\vspace{1.5cm}

\sectiondiv

\vspace{0.5cm}

{\small\itshape 也獻給那些願意像保羅一樣，}

{\small\itshape 為別人說「歸在我的賬上」的人。}

\vspace{0.3cm}

{\footnotesize\itshape Also dedicated to those who, like Paul,}

{\footnotesize\itshape are willing to say "charge it to my account" for others.}

\end{center}
