# California Developer Fast Track Guide — Index & Table of Contents

**A Practitioner's Guide to Residential, Hotel & Modular Development in California**

---

## How to Use This Guide

This guide is organized into **seven parts** covering the complete California development lifecycle — from land use law fundamentals through technical due diligence and financial analysis. Each chapter includes:

- **Overview** — Key concepts and regulatory framework
- **Detailed Content** — Tables, comparisons, and technical specifications
- **Bay Area Case Studies & Lessons Learned** — Real-world examples from Palo Alto, Menlo Park, San Carlos, and surrounding jurisdictions
- **Financial Decision Gateway** — Connecting regulatory content to Appendix G's 3-level financial framework
- **Practitioner Checklist** — Actionable items for each topic
- **Key Takeaway** — Single most important concept to remember

Three thematic threads run through every chapter: (1) the **Financial Decision Gateway** linking regulations to pro forma impacts, (2) the **Bay Area Case Study Method** with 50+ real-world cases, and (3) the **Frontier Builder Lens** connecting content to Appendix F's case studies and technology strategies.
