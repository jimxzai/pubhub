# Business Requirements Document (BRD)
## 三书精读出版系统 (Three Books Deep Reading & Publishing System)

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** December 6, 2025  
**Project Duration:** 7 years (2025-2032)

---

## 1. Executive Summary

The Three Books Deep Reading & Publishing System is a comprehensive, AI-assisted publishing platform designed to enable a 7-year deep reading and commentary project on three classical texts:
- 《孙子兵法》(The Art of War)
- 《资治通鉴》(Zizhi Tongjian - Comprehensive Mirror for Governance)
- 《圣经》(The Bible)

The system will transform daily reading insights into weekly summaries, monthly reports, and ultimately publish three major annotated works that blend classical wisdom with modern AI perspectives.

---

## 2. Business Objectives

### Primary Goals
1. **Content Production**: Generate 1,000+ pages of original annotated commentary across three classical texts
2. **Publishing**: Publish three professionally edited books by 2032
3. **Knowledge Integration**: Create AI-augmented annotations bridging classical and contemporary wisdom
4. **Audience Building**: Establish readership of 10,000+ engaged readers

### Secondary Goals
1. Develop reusable templates and workflows for collaborative writing projects
2. Create a model for AI-assisted scholarly publishing
3. Build a community around classical text reinterpretation
4. Establish intellectual property and digital presence

---

## 3. Project Scope

### Phase 1: Foundation (2025-2026)
- Daily reading and note-taking system operational
- Complete《孙子兵法》(13 sections) deep reading cycle
- Generate 200+ daily notes, 52 weekly summaries
- Produce《孙子·AI注疏》(Art of War: AI Commentary) draft

### Phase 2: Expansion (2026-2029)
- Maintain daily practice across all three texts
- Complete《资治通鉴》(294 chapters) reading cycle
- Produce《资治通鉴·兴衰律36条》(36 Laws of Rise and Fall) draft

### Phase 3: Integration (2029-2031)
- Complete《圣经》(1,189 chapters) reading cycle
- Produce《圣经·12大母题AI注疏》(Bible: 12 Major Themes) draft
- Begin cross-text synthesis

### Phase 4: Publishing (2031-2035)
- Final editing and integration of all three works
- Design and production of physical/digital editions
- Marketing and distribution strategy

## 5. Key Features & Requirements

### 5.1 Daily Workflow
- **Reading Input**: 30 minutes of physical text reading
- **Note Generation**: 300-500 word daily insights
- **Template System**: Auto-generated markdown templates
- **Submission**: Git-based version control

### 5.2 Weekly Aggregation
- **Input**: 7 daily notes per week
- **Output**: 1,000-2,000 word weekly summary
- **Automation**: Scheduled summary generation
- **Review**: Quality check and manual refinement

### 5.3 Monthly Reporting
- **Input**: 4 weekly summaries per month
- **Output**: 5,000 word monthly chapter
- **Format**: PDF preview generation
- **Archive**: Monthly collection for book building

### 5.4 Book Construction
- **Input**: All monthly chapters (36+ per book)
- **Output**: Structured manuscript ready for publication
- **Format**: Markdown → PDF, EPUB, Print-ready versions
- **Metadata**: Table of contents, index, cross-references

### 5.5 Content Management
- **Version Control**: GitHub-based tracking
- **Draft/Published States**: Clear separation of work
- **Automated Workflows**: GitHub Actions for repetitive tasks
- **Search & Discovery**: Ability to find notes across texts and time
